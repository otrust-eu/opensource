Service Description

OTRUST provides three main services:

  • Timestamp: Blockchain timestamping using OpenTimestamps. We create cryptographic proofs that data existed at a specific point in time by anchoring SHA-256 hashes to the Bitcoin blockchain.
  • Signed: Multi-party document signing with blockchain proof. Signers verify document hashes and sign cryptographically, with the final signature package anchored to Bitcoin.
  • Proof: Zero-knowledge identity proofs using ZK-SNARKs. Prove facts about yourself (age, unique identity) without revealing personal data. Proofs are cryptographically verified and can be added to Apple Wallet where supported.

OTRUST is free to use. No account required. No payment needed.

How Timestamps Work

Submission: When you submit a hash, we create a timestamp claim and submit it to OpenTimestamps calendar servers for Bitcoin anchoring.

Confirmation: Bitcoin confirmation typically takes 24-48 hours. Until confirmed, the timestamp proof is pending but still valid.

Verification: Completed proofs can be verified independently using any OpenTimestamps-compatible client. You do not need to trust OTRUST for verification.

How Signed Works

Document Handling: You can choose between temporary upload (encrypted, auto-deleted within 1-12 hours) or zero-knowledge mode where you share the document directly with signers.

Signing Process: Each party receives a unique link to verify the document hash and sign cryptographically. Signatures are collected and the final package is anchored to Bitcoin.

Data Retention: For temporary uploads, documents are encrypted at rest and automatically purged. Signature records (hashes and metadata) are permanent.

Legal Note: OTRUST Signed provides cryptographic proof of signature intent. Signatures are Ed25519 attestations anchored to Bitcoin, proving that parties reviewed and acknowledged a document hash at a specific time. Legal standing varies by jurisdiction and context.

How Proof Works

Identity Verification: You upload an ID document (passport, driver's license, national ID) and take a selfie for liveness detection. All processing happens client-side in your browser.

Zero-Knowledge Proofs: Your data is converted into a ZK-SNARK proof using Groth16. This proves facts about you (e.g., age, unique identity) without revealing the underlying data.

Sybil Resistance: For unique identity proofs, a one-way hash of your ID number (e.g., Swedish personnummer) ensures one proof per person while keeping your actual ID private.

Data Handling: No personal data leaves your browser. Only the cryptographic proof and public commitment are stored. Your ID document and photos are never transmitted to our servers.

Privacy Note: ZK-SNARK proofs are mathematically verified without revealing your personal information. The verifier learns only that the condition is satisfied (e.g., "over 18"), not your actual data.
