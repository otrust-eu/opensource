## Detailed Description

🕐 **Prove When Content Existed**

OTRUST lets you create cryptographic timestamps for any webpage, anchored to the blockchain via OpenTimestamps.

**How it works:**
1. Click the OTRUST icon on any webpage
2. The page content is hashed locally (never uploaded)
3. Your timestamp is anchored to the blockchain
4. Download proof that survives forever

**Features:**
✓ One-click timestamping
✓ Privacy-first: content never leaves your browser
✓ Blockchain-anchored proofs via OpenTimestamps
✓ View your timestamp history
✓ Verify existing timestamps
✓ No account required
✓ 100% free and open source

**Use cases:**
• Prove when you published content
• Document website states for legal purposes
• Archive evidence of online content
• Protect intellectual property
• Research and journalism

**Privacy:**
- Page content is hashed locally in your browser
- Only the SHA-256 hash is sent to the server
- No tracking, no accounts, no personal data
- Open source: https://github.com/otrust-eu

**Technical details:**
- Ed25519 digital signatures
- SHA-256 content hashing
- OpenTimestamps blockchain anchoring
- Proofs independently verifiable
