What is counted

Records include timestamp claims, signing packages, and proof packages. Verified records are records with a completed or active verification state.

  • No artificial baseline is added.
  • Usage events are tracked separately from records.
  • Counts are returned by the public /stats endpoint.

Privacy posture

The product is designed around hashes, signatures, proof metadata, and explicit user-controlled sharing. Raw content is not needed for public verification.

  • File timestamping hashes in the browser.
  • Optional notification emails are stored separately.
  • Identity proof views do not reveal underlying personal data.

Independent verification

OTRUST uses open proof formats where possible and keeps the provider boundary visible. Production can be used hosted or self-hosted from the public repository.

  • OpenTimestamps support for Bitcoin anchoring.
  • Public proof URLs and downloadable receipts.
  • MIT-licensed open source implementation.
