Use cases

Independent verification — free for research, enterprise support for defense and government.

01

Research priority

Hash datasets, notebooks, or manuscript drafts locally. Anchor existence on Bitcoin via OpenTimestamps and share a receipt ID with collaborators or journals.

  • Prove a file existed before a publication date
  • No raw data leaves the browser
  • Verifiers re-hash the original file later

02

Patent & invention evidence

Bind invention disclosures, CAD exports, or lab notes to a cryptographic timestamp before filing. The receipt is independently verifiable years later.

  • Ed25519 signature binds your browser key
  • Bitcoin anchoring via OTS (~1–2 hours)
  • Downloadable certificate when confirmed

Partner hosted Auth

  • Branded hosted step without replacing eID providers

04

Defense & Government

Timestamp logs, evidence, and documents locally for verifiable chain-of-custody. Prove existence and integrity without ever leaving your classified or air-gapped network. Sovereign, zero-knowledge proofs for audit trails, supply-chain provenance, and classified document workflows.

  • Zero-knowledge proofs of prior existence — no data leaves your perimeter
  • Bitcoin-anchored immutability outside any national CA or single point of failure
  • Full self-host / air-gapped deployments for high-security environments
  • Support for evidence bundles, Merkle proofs, and long-term verifiability
  • Enterprise SLAs, integration support, compliance assistance (e.g. NATO/FMV), and dedicated defense deployments available
