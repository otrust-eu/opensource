Research priority

Patent & invention evidence

Bind invention disclosures, CAD exports, or lab notes to a cryptographic timestamp before filing. The receipt is independently verifiable years later.

  • Ed25519 signature binds your browser key
  • Bitcoin anchoring via OTS (~1–2 hours)
  • Downloadable certificate when confirmed
